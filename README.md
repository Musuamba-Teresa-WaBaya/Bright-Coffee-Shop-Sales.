# Bright-Coffee-Shop-Sales.
Analysis for the CEO to understand the sales at Bright Learn coffee Shop for the last 6 months 

### Problem Statement 
The sales at the shop have been declining in the past 6 months and the CEO wants to understand the cause of the decline in sales. I m tasked to do diagnostics analysis to undertand the decline in revenue

### Aim 
The aim of this project is ot provide detailed insights on wha causes the decline in revenue

### Objectives 
In order to achieve this aim, the following objectives must be met:

Brainstorming: Use Miro to identify potential root causes and map out data relationships.

Project Planning: Use Canva to design a structured project roadmap and visual timeline.

Data Processing: Use Databricks (Spark SQL/Python) to clean, transform, and analyze the sales datasets.

### Summary of results 
The sales dropped by 50% in january as compared to December. This could be due to seasonal changes.
